# Open Questions

Open questions are not implementation permission. Resolve them through explicit product or technical decisions and update the relevant source-of-truth docs.

## Input and LLM

- How much free-text input, if any, should be part of the MVP UX?
- Should an optional LLM parser ship as beta functionality in MVP, or be postponed until after the manual core is validated?

## Information design

- How much numerical detail should be visible to players?
- Should formulas remain hidden, be partially visible, or be available through advanced tooltips?
- What level of visual polish is sufficient for the first external beta?

## Content and identity

- Which archetypes should be playable in the first public beta?
- What final naming and identity conventions should be used for the world, turn unit, rival projects, player polity, and UI labels?

Naming and identity beyond the title **Ratio Status** are intentionally deferred until beta-user feedback. Development may use clear placeholder terms, but they remain provisional and must not become implicit final product decisions.
